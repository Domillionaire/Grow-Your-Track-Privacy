Privacy Policy — Grow Your Track

Effective Date: 2026-06-14

Grow Your Track respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is processed when you use the app and how it is used to operate core features such as AI tools and in-app purchases.

Summary

  • No account or sign-up is required.
  • Projects, arrangements, and recordings are stored locally on your device.
  • The app does not use advertising SDKs.
  • AI features send your text prompts to our server and to OpenAI to generate results.
  • In-app purchases are verified by our server to ensure credits are granted correctly.

Information You Provide

Project Inputs
You may enter text such as genre, mood, energy, lyric direction, and lyrics. These inputs are used to generate lyric results inside the app.

Feedback (Optional)
If you use the in-app feedback feature, we receive the message text you submit. Providing personal information is optional.

Audio & Microphone

The app requests microphone access only when you choose to record voice notes or vocal takes.
Recordings are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share or export them.

AI Features and Processing (OpenAI)

When you use AI-powered features (such as lyrics generation), relevant text inputs are sent to our server and then forwarded to OpenAI to generate a response.

We do not require you to provide personal identifying information (such as name or email) to use AI features.

Please do not include sensitive personal information in AI prompt fields.

OpenAI may retain API data for up to 30 days for safety monitoring and abuse prevention, after which it is deleted. OpenAI does not use API data to train its models.

In-App Purchases and Credits

Grow Your Track offers optional in-app purchases that provide credits used for certain app features.

When a purchase is made, the transaction is verified with Apple to confirm the purchase is legitimate before credits are added.

To manage credit balances and prevent duplicate purchase processing, the app stores limited purchase-related information on our server.

This may include:

  • Apple transaction identifiers
  • Product identifiers
  • Credits granted
  • Transaction timestamps

This information is used only to verify purchases and maintain accurate credit balances.

Device Identifier

To maintain credit balances without requiring user accounts, the app generates a random device identifier that is stored securely on your device.

This identifier is sent with certain requests to our server so that purchases and credits can be associated with your device.

This identifier does not contain personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

Data Storage

On-device:
Projects, arrangements, lyrics, and recordings are stored locally on your device.

On our servers:
We do not maintain user accounts and do not intentionally store user projects, lyrics, or recordings.

Limited operational data may be stored to operate the credit system and verify in-app purchases. This includes the device identifier and purchase verification data described above.

Operational logs:
Our backend is hosted on Replit. Like most hosting providers, server infrastructure may generate basic operational logs (such as request metadata and error logs) for reliability and security purposes. These logs are not used for advertising or user tracking.

Tracking and Advertising

Grow Your Track does not include advertising SDKs and does not use third-party analytics tracking SDKs.

Sharing

If you choose to share or export a final mix using iOS sharing options, that content is shared through services you select and is subject to those services’ privacy practices.

Children’s Privacy

Grow Your Track is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the effective date will be updated.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, please use the in-app Send Feedback option.
